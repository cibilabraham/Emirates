Anshad Aziz

I'm Anshad Aash Aziz
I have accomplished figures in the fashion and entertainment industry, both in India and internationally. With extensive experience in modeling, I have collaborated with many leading Indian and global fashion brands, as well as prestigious fashion houses like Emirates modeling and Diva model management. Expertise extends to choreographing major shows for renowned events such as Dubai Fashion Week and Indian Fashion Week, i have worked alongside international designers and brands. In addition to my works in the fashion world, I have also made a mark as an Indian reporter with the esteemed Miami-based 'Fashion Face' magazine, introducing Indian fashion trends and models to a global audience. Besides my successful modeling career, I'm a professional dancer skilled in both Classical and Western forms, showcasing my versatile talents in the industry.
